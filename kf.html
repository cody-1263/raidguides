King's Fall

Equipment recommendations. They are not absolute and only serve a purpose of giving you a general guess about what you might want in every encounter.

1. Totems

UNSTOPPABLE CHAMPIONS
Close and medium combat distances
Small and medium enemies
Don't forget to adjust your gear to include anti-unstoppable tools. Depending on available anti-champ mods, you might want to go for other weapon types
Primary: Solar HC / Unstop weapon
For small adds and solar-shielded wizards
Special: Shotgun / Fusion rifle
Special weapons to help deal with orange-bars and champions
Heavy: Machinegun
For orange bars standing in the distance

2. Warpriest

Close combat distances
Small and medium enemies
Primary: Trinity Ghoul / SMGs / Hand cannons
Primary weapons to clear adds
Special: Shotgun / Fusion / GL
Special weapons to help with add clear and orange-bars
Heavy: Linear Fusion Rifles
For boss damage

3. Golgoroth

Close combat distances
Small and medium enemies
Primary: Trinity Ghoul / SMGs / Hand cannons
Primary weapons to clear adds
Special: Arbalest / Sniper
For boss mechanics or additional DPS ammo, those will work for both purposes
Heavy: Linear Fusion Rifle / Machinegun
LFR for damage or LMG for defending from axion darts

4. Daughters

Close combat distances
Small and medium enemies
Primary: Trinity Ghoul / Hand cannons / Auto rifles
Primary weapons to clear adds + occasional sniper vandals
Special: Sniper / GL
Special weapons to help with add clear and enemy snipers
Heavy: Rockets
For boss damage
