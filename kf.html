1. Totems

UNSTOPPABLE CHAMPIONS
Close and medium combat distances
Small and medium enemies

Special: Grenade Launchers
Special weapons to help deal with orange-bars and champions
Heavy: Machineguns / Rockets
Again, destroy hive knights and other adds quicker
