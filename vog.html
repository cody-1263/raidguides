Vault of Glass

Equipment recommendations. They are not absolute and only serve a purpose of giving you a general guess about what you might want in every encounter.

1. Confluxes

OVERLOAD CHAMPIONS
Close and medium combat distances
Small and big enemies
Don't forget to adjust your gear to include anti-overload tools. Depending on available anti-champ mods, you might want to go for other weapon types
Primary: SMGs / Hand cannons / Auto rifles
Primary weapons to destroy goblins with
Special: GLs / Fusions / Shotguns
Some additional firepower to deal with minotaurs and groups of enemies quickly
Heavy: Rockets
For wyvern-class mini-bosses and champions

2. Oracles

Close and far combat distances
Small and medium enemies
Primary: Auto rifles / Pulse rifles / SMGs
Primary weapons to destroy goblins and, optionally, snipers with
Special: GLs / Fusions / Snipers
Some additional firepower to deal with minotaurs and groups of enemies, or destroy oracles
Heavy: Xenophage / Rockets
XENOPHAGE is strongly recommended for encounter mechanics and snipers. If you don't have it, then take something else

3. Templar

Medium DPS distance
Small enemies
Primary: Whatever
Primary weapons to kill a bunch of goblins and harpies
Special: Snipers / Fusion Rifles
Spare ammo for DPS
Heavy: Linear Fusion Rifles
For Boss DPS. The crit spot is easy to hit as it is, but you can add Divinity to make it even better

4. Gatekeepers

OVERLOAD CHAMPIONS
Close and medium combat distances
Small and big enemies
Don't forget to adjust your gear to include anti-overload tools. Depending on available anti-champ mods, you might want to go for other weapon types
Primary: SMGs / Auto Rifles / Hand cannons
Primary weapons to destroy goblins with
Special: Witherhoard / Ager's Scepter / Grenade Launchers
To deal with groups of enemies faster
Heavy: Rockets
For mini-bosses and champions

5. Atheon

All combat distances
Small and medium enemies
Primary: Scout rifles
There isn't a lot of enemies to defeat, but your targets are far away most of the time
Special: Sniper rifles
Your main tool to destroy oracles with
Heavy: Linear Fusion Rifles
Again, boss DPS, crit spot, Divinity
