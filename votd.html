Vow of the Disciple

These are our equipment recommendations. They are not absolute, but rather are supposed to give you a general idea of which weapons to use in each encounter.

1. Acquisition

UNSTOPPABLE CHAMPIONS
close combat distances
redbars, elites, majors and champions
Don't forget to adjust your gear according to the seasonal unstoppable mods. Depending on the available anti-champion mods, you may want to select different weapon types than those listed here.
Primary: any high firerate primary weapon / any unstoppable weapon
Your primary weapon will be mainly used to stun champions and to perform encounter mechanics.
Special: Grenade Launchers / Fusion Rifles with Reservoir Burst
Your special weapon will be doing most of the add-clear.
Heavy: Rocket Launchers with Tracking Module / Linear Fusion Rifles / Swords
Your heavy weapon will be used to deal with the champions and majors. Eager Edge can be used for that as well, while doubling as a movement tool.

2. Collection

close and medium combat distances
redbars and elites
Primary: Hand Cannons / Auto Rifles
Your primary weapon will be mainly used to shoot darkness darts and to perform encounter mechanics.
Special: Grenade Launchers / Fusion Rifles with Reservoir Burst
Your special weapon will be doing most of the add-clear.
Heavy: Linear Fusion Rifles
Your heavy weapon will be used to damage the boss.

3. Exhibition

OVERLOAD CHAMPIONS
close combat distances
redbards, elites, majors and champions
Don't forget to adjust your gear according to the seasonal unstoppable mods. Depending on the available anti-champion mods, you may want to select different weapon types than those listed here.
Primary: Osteo Striga / any overload weapon / Trinity Ghoul
Your primary weapon will be used mainly to stun champions, perform encounter mechanics and as a backup for clearing adds.
Special: Grenade Launchers / Fusion Rifles / Shotguns
Your special weapon will either be used for add-clear or to kill majors and champions.
Heavy: Machine Guns / Swords
Your heavy weapon will either be used for add-clear or to kill majors and champions.

4. Dominion

close and medium combat distances
redbars and majors
Primary: Auto Rifles / Hand Cannons / Submachine Guns
Your primary weapon will be used for encounter mechanics and add-clear
Special: Fusion Rifles / Grenade Launchers / Sniper Rifles
Your special weapon will be used for add-clear, encounter mechanics or as an additional boss damage option.
Heavy: Linear Fusion Rifles / Rocket Launchers
Your heavy weapon will be used to damage the boss.
